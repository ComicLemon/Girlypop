About FortuneTale

FortuneTale is a webcomic made by the artist ComicLemon. It is separated into a few different comic threads some of the most prominent being:

  • AskFortune
  • FortuneTale
  • Ghost's Story

AskFortune is a comic series that follows a series of asks that were sent in by fans of series.

FortuneTale is another comic series that follows Fortune!Sans' backstory.
