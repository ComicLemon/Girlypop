About FortuneTale

FortuneTale is a webcomic made by the artist ComicLemon. It is separated into a few different comic threads some of the main being:

  • Ask Fortune
  • FortuneTale
  • Ghost's Story

AskFortune is a comic series that follows a series of asks that were sent in by fans of series.

FortuneTale is another comic series that follows Fortune!Sans' backstory.

Finally, Ghost's Story follows Ghost!Sans' baackstory and the inevatible end to Fortune and Ghost's familial relationship.

FortuneTale specifically follows the story of Fortune!Sans.

Fortune

Fortune is the main character of both FortuneTale and Ask!Fortune.

Fortune was first introduced on the 10th of June, 2024 when ComicLemon posted the first digital art drawing of him on Tumblr.
